## What is MathPhysics?

MathPhysics is an Android app that transforms how students learn mathematics and physics. Instead of static textbook diagrams, every concept is explained through interactive animations that bring formulas, theorems, and physical phenomena to life. Available in both English and Bengali, it serves students across South Asia and beyond.

## Animation-First Learning

The app is built around a custom animation engine that renders smooth, interactive visualizations. Students can see how a projectile follows a parabolic path, watch vectors compose in real time, or manipulate geometric proofs with their fingers. This visual approach makes abstract concepts concrete and memorable.

## Comprehensive and Accessible

Covering grades 9 through 12, MathPhysics includes lessons, practice problems, and progress tracking for both mathematics and physics. The bilingual support ensures that language is never a barrier to understanding STEM concepts, making quality education accessible to Bengali-speaking students worldwide.
