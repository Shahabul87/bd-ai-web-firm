## What is TaxoMind?

TaxoMind is an AI-powered learning platform that goes beyond traditional course completion tracking. Instead of measuring how many lessons you have finished, TaxoMind evaluates *how deeply* you understand the material by mapping your progress across all six stages of Bloom&apos;s Taxonomy: Remember, Understand, Apply, Analyze, Evaluate, and Create.

## How It Works

The platform uses an intelligent evaluation engine to assess your responses, projects, and interactions. Based on your demonstrated cognitive level, it generates a personalized learning path that challenges you at exactly the right difficulty. As you progress, real-time dashboards show your growth across each cognitive dimension, helping you and your educators identify strengths and areas for improvement.

## Why TaxoMind?

Most learning platforms treat education as a checklist. TaxoMind treats it as a journey of cognitive development. Whether you are a self-learner pushing toward mastery, an educator seeking deeper student insights, or an institution implementing evidence-based assessment, TaxoMind provides the tools to make learning measurable, personalized, and effective.
