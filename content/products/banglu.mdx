## What is Banglu?

Banglu lets you type in Bangla using the English keyboard you already have. You write words the way they sound — phonetically — and Banglu converts them into correct Bengali script in real time.

## How It Works

As you type romanized Bangla, Banglu's conversion engine applies the spelling and character-joining rules of Bengali to produce properly formed script — complete with conjuncts and vowel signs. When you're happy with your text, copy it and paste it wherever you need it.

## Why Banglu?

Typing Bangla usually means learning a dedicated keyboard layout or fighting with clunky tools. Banglu removes that barrier: if you can type the sounds, you can write fluent Bangla — instantly, in your browser, on any device.

## বাংলু এডিটর — the Desktop App

For longer writing — letters, assignments, posts — the desktop app turns your computer into a Bangla writing machine. Words form in Bangla live at the cursor as you type, a floating popup offers alternatives (pick with the 1–6 keys), and you can click any word on the page to correct it later. Documents save as plain .txt with crash-proof autosave, export to Word with perfect conjuncts, and print straight to PDF. It ships with a built-in শিখুন tutorial covering the full phonetic mapping, plus a system-wide mini converter (Ctrl+Shift+B / ⌘⇧B) that works over any app. Everything runs 100% offline — the complete dictionary is bundled and nothing you type ever leaves your machine.
