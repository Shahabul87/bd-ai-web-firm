## What is FinCoach AI?

FinCoach AI is a personal finance companion that uses artificial intelligence to help you take control of your money. Available on Android, it combines budget tracking, expense analysis, and investment insights into one intuitive app that learns your financial habits and coaches you toward your goals.

## Smart Financial Guidance

The app goes beyond simple expense tracking. Using ML Kit and custom AI models, FinCoach AI analyzes your spending patterns, identifies areas where you can save, and provides personalized recommendations. Whether you are saving for a vacation, paying off debt, or starting to invest, the app creates a tailored roadmap with milestones to keep you on track.

## Built with Modern Android

FinCoach AI is built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose, following Material Design 3 guidelines for a beautiful, accessible experience. Firebase powers real-time sync, authentication, and cloud functions, ensuring your financial data is always secure and available across devices.
