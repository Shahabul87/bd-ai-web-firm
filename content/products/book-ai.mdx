## What is Book-AI?

Book-AI is an interactive learning platform for the mathematics behind artificial intelligence. Instead of static diagrams and walls of notation, every concept is presented as something you can see and manipulate — vectors you can rotate, matrices whose transformations you can watch unfold, and 3D structures you can turn in space.

## How It Works

Each concept is taught in layers: first the plain-language intuition, then a fully worked numerical example, and finally an interactive viewer where you change the inputs and watch the results update live. This "read it, work it, play with it" loop turns abstract ideas into something you actually understand.

## Why Book-AI?

Most people don't struggle with AI because they can't code — they struggle because the underlying math stays abstract. Book-AI closes that gap by making linear algebra, calculus, machine learning, and even crystallography visual, interactive, and genuinely intuitive.
