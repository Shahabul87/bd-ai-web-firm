## What is TaxoMind Schools?

TaxoMind Schools is the institutional edition of our AI-powered cognitive learning platform, designed specifically for schools, colleges, and training centers. It brings the power of Bloom&apos;s Taxonomy-based assessment to the classroom, giving educators the tools to track not just what students know, but how deeply they understand it.

## Built for Institutions

Unlike the individual platform, TaxoMind Schools includes classroom management, batch assessment, and administrative reporting features. Teachers can create classes, assign curriculum-aligned assessments, and view real-time analytics on student cognitive progress. Administrators get a bird&apos;s-eye view of institutional learning outcomes.

## Measurable Learning Outcomes

Every assessment, assignment, and interaction is mapped to a cognitive level. This means schools can demonstrate measurable learning outcomes to accreditation bodies, parents, and stakeholders with concrete data on student cognitive growth over time.
