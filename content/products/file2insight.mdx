## What is File2Insight?

File2Insight turns your documents into answers. Upload a file — a PDF, a report, a data export — and the platform reads it, understands it, and lets you get what you need out of it in seconds instead of hours.

## How It Works

Once your file is uploaded, File2Insight uses AI language models to interpret its content. You can request a summary, ask questions about specific details, or extract the exact information you're after — all in plain language, with answers grounded in the actual document.

## Why File2Insight?

The information you need is often buried in documents that are too long to read end-to-end. File2Insight removes that friction, letting you move straight from "here's a file" to "here's what it says and what it means."
