## The Traditional Cost Problem

Software development has always been expensive. A typical custom web application costs between $50,000 and $200,000 when built the traditional way, with most of that budget going toward developer hours spent writing boilerplate code, configuring infrastructure, and implementing patterns that have been solved thousands of times before. For small and mid-sized businesses, these costs put custom software out of reach entirely.

## How AI Agents Change the Economics

AI-powered development fundamentally changes this equation. Our AI agents generate production-grade code for the repetitive, well-understood parts of software development &mdash; database schemas, API endpoints, authentication flows, form validation, and responsive UI components. This is not auto-generated template code. These are tailored implementations that follow your specific business logic and architecture decisions.

## Where Human Engineers Add Value

The savings come from reallocation, not replacement. Our senior engineers focus their time on the work that actually requires human judgment: system architecture, complex business logic, security review, performance optimization, and user experience design. By eliminating the hours spent on mechanical coding tasks, we compress timelines from months to weeks while our engineers spend more of their time on the decisions that matter most.

## Real Numbers

Across our portfolio, AI-powered development consistently delivers 60% to 80% cost reduction compared to traditional agency pricing. A project that would cost $100,000 at a conventional shop typically comes in between $20,000 and $40,000 with our approach &mdash; with the same or better quality, because our engineers spend proportionally more time on architecture and review rather than typing code.
